Steps to Make Ultimate Cranberry Jalapeno Cream Cheese Dip Delicious

To get started with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can cook Cranberry Jalapeno Cream Cheese Dip using 9 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Cranberry Jalapeno Cream Cheese Dip:

  • Make ready ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • Take 10 ounces dried sweetened cranberries
  • Get ½ cup white sugar, or to taste
  • Take ½ cup water, or more as needed
  • Take 2 small jalapeno peppers, seeded and finely diced
  • Make ready 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Make ready 2 teaspoons dried cilantro
  • Prepare ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Make ready 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened

Step by Step to cook Cranberry Jalapeno Cream Cheese Dip:

  1. Heat a saucepan over medium heat; add oil. Stir cranberries into hot oil and cook until cranberries absorb most of oil, 5 to 10 minutes. Mix sugar, water, jalapeno peppers, lemon juice, cilantro, and salt into cranberries; cook until reduced and softened, 10 to 15 more minutes. Add more water if needed.
  2. Pulse cranberry mixture in a food processor or blender until very smooth; cool completely.
  3. Stir cooled cranberry mixture and cream cheese together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
