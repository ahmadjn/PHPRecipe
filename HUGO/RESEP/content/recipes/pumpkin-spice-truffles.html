How to Prepare Ultimate Pumpkin Spice Truffles Tasty

To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook Pumpkin Spice Truffles using 3 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Spice Truffles:

  • Take 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
  • Get 1 (24 ounce) package white baking chocolate, broken in half
  • Take 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Instructions to make Pumpkin Spice Truffles:

  1. Place cream cheese into a medium bowl and beat using a hand mixer until fluffy.
  2. Set 1/2 the white chocolate aside and place other 1/2 into a medium microwave-safe bowl. Microwave for 30 seconds and stir. Continue to microwave in 30-second intervals until chocolate is completely melted and smooth.
  3. Pour melted chocolate into whipped cream cheese. Blend using a hand mixer until mixture is well blended, about 2 minutes. Add pumpkin pie spice and continue to mix until well blended, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Form chocolate mixture into small balls using a spoon or melon baller and place on a baking sheet lined with wax paper. Place balls in the refrigerator until firm enough to retain their shape when dipped in warm chocolate, at least 1 hour.
  5. Melt remaining white chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl in a microwave in 30-second increments until completely melted. Dip each ball entirely into melted chocolate; return to wax paper and let cool until set, 20 to 30 minutes.
