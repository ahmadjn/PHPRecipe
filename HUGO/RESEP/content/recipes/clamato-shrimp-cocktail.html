The ingredients needed to make Clamato Shrimp Cocktail:

  • Prepare 2 ½ pounds large cooked (grilled) shrimp, cut in half
  • Prepare ½ cup lemon juice
  • Get 3 large tomatoes, diced
  • Get 1 large white onion, diced
  • Take ½ cup Sunkist® or Crush® orange-flavor soda
  • Make ready 6 ounces ketchup
  • Prepare 2 cups Clamato® Original or Picante flavor
  • Get Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Get 6 fluid ounces Worcestershire sauce
  • Take 3 ounces hot pepper sauce
  • Take 2 avocados, cut into cubes

Steps to cook Clamato Shrimp Cocktail:

  1. Mix shrimp, with lemon juice, tomatoes, onion, Sunkist&reg; or Crush&reg; orange flavor, Ketchup, Clamato&reg; Original or Picante flavor, Salt &amp; pepper to taste, Worcestershire Sauce, hot sauce (to taste) and refrigerate for 30 minutes. Before serving add avocado.
