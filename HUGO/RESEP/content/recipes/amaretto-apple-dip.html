  • Make ready 1 ½ cups brown sugar
  • Get 1 cup powdered sugar
  • Make ready 4 tablespoons amaretto liqueur
  • Make ready 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • Prepare 1 ¼ tablespoons milk

Steps to make Amaretto Apple Dip:

  1. Combine cream cheese, brown sugar, powdered sugar, amaretto, vanilla extract, and milk in a food processor. Process until creamy.
