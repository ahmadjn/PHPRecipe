Steps to Make Ultimate Gluten-Free Vegan Granola Bars Tasty

To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have Gluten-Free Vegan Granola Bars using 14 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Gluten-Free Vegan Granola Bars:

  • Make ready cooking spray
  • Take 1 ½ cups almond butter
  • Prepare 1 cup honey
  • Take ⅔ cup coconut oil
  • Make ready 3 cups gluten-free rolled oats
  • Prepare 1 ½ cups crispy rice cereal
  • Take 1 cup chopped almonds
  • Make ready 1 cup dried cranberries
  • Take ⅔ cup vegan chocolate chips
  • Make ready ½ cup chia seeds
  • Prepare ½ cup flaxseed meal
  • Make ready ½ cup sesame seeds
  • Prepare ½ cup cacao nibs
  • Prepare ⅓ cup unsweetened dried coconut

Step by Step to make Gluten-Free Vegan Granola Bars:

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Line a 10x15-inch rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil, leaving an overhang on 2 sides. Grease with cooking spray.
  2. Combine almond butter, honey, and coconut oil in a saucepan. Heat over low heat until melted and easily blended, about 5 minutes.
  3. Mix oats, crispy rice cereal, almonds, cranberries, chocolate chips, chia seeds, flaxseed meal, sesame seeds, cacao nibs, and coconut in a large bowl. Pour in almond butter mixture; toss until fully mixed.
  4. Press mixture into the baking sheet with moistened hands until it is very compact.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 15 minutes. Allow to cool completely, about 20 minutes. Lift from the baking sheet and slicing into 30 bars.
