The ingredients needed to make Corn Salsa with Avocado:

  • Prepare 2 large avocados, diced
  • Get 1 large tomato, diced
  • Prepare 1 medium onion, diced
  • Prepare ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Get 2 ears fresh corn
  • Prepare 1 lime
  • Prepare ½ cup olive oil
  • Get 3 tablespoons white vinegar
  • Prepare 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • Take ½ tablespoon salt
  • Take ¼ tablespoon ground black pepper

Step by Step to cook Corn Salsa with Avocado:

  1. Combine avocados, tomato, onion, and cilantro in a large bowl. Cut corn kernels from the cobs and add to the same bowl.
  2. Cut the lime and squeeze juice into the salsa mixture; add olive oil, vinegar, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
