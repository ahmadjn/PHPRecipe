The ingredients needed to make Quick Thousand Island Dressing:

  • Make ready 1 cup mayonnaise
  • Prepare ¼ cup ketchup
  • Take ¼ cup sweet pickle relish, or more to taste
  • Take 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Take 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish, or more to taste
  • Prepare ½ teaspoon paprika, or more to taste

Instructions to cook Quick Thousand Island Dressing:

  1. Combine mayonnaise, ketchup, pickle relish, Worcestershire sauce, horseradish, and paprika in a bowl; mix well. Cover with plastic wrap and chill before serving.
