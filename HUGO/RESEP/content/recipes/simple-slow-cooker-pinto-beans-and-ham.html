The ingredients needed to make Simple Slow Cooker Pinto Beans and Ham:

  • Get 14 cups water, divided
  • Make ready 2 cups dried pinto beans
  • Get 1 ham bone with meat
  • Get 1 large red onion, chopped
  • Make ready 2 teaspoons garlic, minced
  • Get 1 teaspoon ham base (such as Better than Bouillon速)
  • Make ready 1 dash hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco速), or to taste
  • Get 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps to make Simple Slow Cooker Pinto Beans and Ham:

  1. Bring 8 cups water and pinto beans to a boil in a large pot. Remove pot from heat, cover, and let stand for 1 hour. Drain and rinse before using.
  2. Transfer pinto beans to a slow cooker. Add ham bone, red onion, minced garlic, and ham base. Pour in remaining 6 cups of water, or enough to cover the beans.
  3. Cool on Low until beans are tender, about 8 hours.
  4. Remove the ham bone, shred meat, and add back to the beans. Discard the bone. Serve hot, seasoned with hot sauce, salt, and pepper.
