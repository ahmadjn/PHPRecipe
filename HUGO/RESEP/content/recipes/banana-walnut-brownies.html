The ingredients needed to make Banana Walnut Brownies:

  • Make ready 1 teaspoon shortening, or as needed
  • Take 2 cups white sugar
  • Take 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Prepare ¾ cup baking cocoa
  • Get ½ cup melted butter
  • Take ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • Prepare ¼ cup dark cocoa
  • Make ready 2 eggs
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons water
  • Prepare 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  • Get 1 ripe banana, sliced

Steps to cook Banana Walnut Brownies:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x12-inch baking pan with shortening.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, baking cocoa, butter, walnuts, dark cocoa, eggs, water, vanilla extract, and salt together in a bowl until batter is smooth. Pour the mixture into the baking pan; top with bananas.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 30 minutes.
