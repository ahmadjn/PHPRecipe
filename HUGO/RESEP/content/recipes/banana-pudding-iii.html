Step-by-Step Guide to Make Homemade Banana Pudding III Tasty

To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook Banana Pudding III using 7 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Banana Pudding III:

  • Take 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • Get 1 ½ cups cold water
  • Prepare 1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • Take 2 cups heavy cream
  • Take 3 bananas, sliced
  • Get ½ cup lemon juice
  • Make ready 36 vanilla wafers

Steps to create Banana Pudding III:

  1. In a medium bowl, stir together condensed milk and water. Beat in pudding mix until smooth. Chill in refrigerator 5 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip cream until stiff peaks form. Fold whipped cream into chilled pudding mixture.
  3. Dip sliced bananas in lemon juice. Shake off excess.
  4. In a 2 1/2 quart serving bowl, spoon 1 cup pudding mixture. Top with one-third each of the wafers, bananas and remaining pudding. Repeat layers twice. Chill until serving.
