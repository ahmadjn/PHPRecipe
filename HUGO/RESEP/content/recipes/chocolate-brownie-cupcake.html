Recipe of Homemade Chocolate Brownie Cupcake Delicious

To get started with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have Chocolate Brownie Cupcake using 9 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Brownie Cupcake:

  • Get 2 cups butter
  • Get 1 (8 ounce) package semisweet chocolate (such as Baker's®
  • Get 1 (7 ounce) package shredded coconut (such as Baker's® Angel Flake®)
  • Prepare 8 eggs
  • Make ready 3 cups white sugar
  • Get 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Get 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • Make ready ½ teaspoon salt
  • Take 2 cups walnuts, or more to taste

Step by Step to create Chocolate Brownie Cupcake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 4 muffin tins or line with paper liners.
  2. Combine butter and chocolate in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Cook and stir until melted, about 5 minutes; mix in coconut.
  3. Beat eggs with an electric mixer in a bowl. Mix in sugar, flour, vanilla extract, and salt. Fold in walnuts. Fold in chocolate mixture. Spoon batter into prepared muffin tins until 2/3 full.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 18 to 20 minutes.
