The ingredients needed to make Best Ever Classic Banana Bread:

  • Prepare 3 large ripe bananas, mashed (about 1 1/2 cups)
  • Get 1 cup Country Crock® Spread
  • Take ¾ cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • Take 2 large eggs
  • Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Take 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Prepare 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Get 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Make ready ¼ teaspoon salt

Step by Step to make Best Ever Classic Banana Bread:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Brush 9 x 5-inch loaf pan with additional Country Crock&reg; Spread; set aside.
  2. Combine mashed bananas, Country Crock&reg; Spread, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla in large bowl with wooden spoon. Stir in remaining ingredients until blended. Spoon into prepared pan.
  3. Bake for 1 hour to 1 hour 10 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 20 minutes on wire rack; remove from pan and cool completely.
