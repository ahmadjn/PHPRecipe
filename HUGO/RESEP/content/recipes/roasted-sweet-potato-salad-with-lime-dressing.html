The ingredients needed to make Roasted Sweet Potato Salad with Lime Dressing:

  • Get 2 large sweet potatoes, cut into 1/4-inch cubes
  • Make ready 1 large onion, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
  • Make ready 1 tablespoon oil
  • Take 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
  • Make ready 1 large tomato, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
  • Get ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Get 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger root
  • Get 1 lime, juiced
  • Get 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps to make Roasted Sweet Potato Salad with Lime Dressing:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Combine sweet potatoes and onion in a large bowl. Add oil and toss to coat. Spread onto a large baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until crisp-tender, about 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly, about 10 minutes.
  4. While the vegetables are cooling, combine garbanzo beans, tomato, and cilantro in a large bowl. Combine ginger, lime juice, and garlic in a small bowl.
  5. Add cooled vegetables to the garbanzo bean mixture, pour lime dressing over top, and toss to combine. Chill in the refrigerator until flavors have combined, about 1 hour.
