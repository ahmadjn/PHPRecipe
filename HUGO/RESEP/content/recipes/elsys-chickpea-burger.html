Simple Way to Cook Quick Elsys Chickpea Burger Tasty

To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook Elsys Chickpea Burger using 12 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Elsys Chickpea Burger:

  • Prepare 2 cups dried chickpeas (garbanzo beans)
  • Make ready water to cover
  • Prepare ½ cup roughly chopped cilantro leaves and stems
  • Make ready 2 medium eggs
  • Take 5 cloves garlic, peeled
  • Get 3 tablespoons chickpea flour
  • Get 2 tablespoons tahini
  • Make ready 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  • Get 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Make ready ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • Get ½ cup vegetable oil, or as needed

Steps to make Elsys Chickpea Burger:

  1. Place the chickpeas into a large container and cover with several inches of cool water; let stand 8 hours to overnight.
  2. Drain water from beans and transfer beans to a pot; cover with water. Bring water to a boil; reduce heat and simmer until beans are tender, about 1 hour. Cool beans until easily handled.
  3. Combine chickpeas, cilantro, eggs, garlic, chickpea flour, tahini, coriander, salt, cumin, and chili powder in a blender; blend until well mixed but still chunky. Form mixture into patties.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat; fry patties until golden and crisp, about 3 minutes per side.
