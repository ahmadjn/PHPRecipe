The ingredients needed to make Tuscan Chicken Skillet with Kale Sun-Dried Tomatoes:

  • Get 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons I Can't Believe It's Not Butter!® Spread, divided
  • Make ready 4 cups lightly packed chopped kale leaves
  • Prepare 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • Prepare 1 package Knorr® Rice Sides™ - Cheddar Broccoli
  • Prepare ¼ cup sun-dried tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • Take Lemon wedges

Steps to make Tuscan Chicken Skillet with Kale Sun-Dried Tomatoes:

  1. Season chicken, if desired, with salt and pepper. Melt 1 tablespoon Spread in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and cook chicken, stirring occasionally, until thoroughly cooked, about 5 minutes. Remove and set aside
  2. Melt remaining 1 tablespoon Spread in same skillet over medium-high heat and cook kale and garlic, stirring occasionally, until kale wilts and garlic is fragrant, about 3 minutes. Add 2 cups water and bring to a boil. Stir in Knorr&reg; Rice Sides&trade; - Chicken Broccoli and sun-dried tomatoes and prepare according to package directions.
  3. Stir in chicken. Serve with lemon wedges and, if desired, garnish with toasted pine nuts.
