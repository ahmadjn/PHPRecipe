The ingredients needed to make Nothing Id Rather Eat Quinoa Patties:

  • Prepare 5 cups water
  • Get 2 ½ cups quinoa
  • Take 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Take 2 onions, chopped
  • Prepare salt to taste
  • Get 2 large sweet potatoes
  • Get 4 eggs
  • Make ready ½ cup tahini
  • Take ½ cup kefir
  • Get ½ cup ground flax seeds
  • Get ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese, or more to taste
  • Prepare ¼ cup sesame seeds
  • Make ready ¼ cup sunflower seeds
  • Make ready ¼ cup coarsely chopped pumpkin seeds, or more to taste
  • Make ready 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Make ready 1 tablespoon all-purpose vegetable seasoning
  • Get 1 tablespoon raw apple cider vinegar (such as Bragg®)
  • Prepare salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Make ready ¾ cup frozen corn kernels, thawed
  • Make ready ½ cup spelt flour, or more as needed
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons butter
  • Prepare 24 thin slices Cheddar cheese

Step by Step to cook Nothing Id Rather Eat Quinoa Patties:

  1. Bring water and quinoa to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until quinoa is soft, 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions and salt; cook and stir until tender, 5 to 8 minutes.
  3. Place sweet potatoes on a microwave-safe plate; prick all over with a fork. Cook on high, turning once, until tender, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool until easily handled, about 5 minutes.
  4. Peel sweet potatoes and place flesh in a bowl. Add eggs, tahini, kefir, flax seeds, Parmesan cheese, sesame seeds, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, soy sauce, vegetable seasoning, vinegar, salt, and pepper; beat with an electric mixer until well blended. Stir in quinoa, onions, and corn.
  5. Stir enough spelt flour into the sweet potato mixture until it barely holds together.
  6. Melt 1/4 teaspoon butter on a griddle over medium heat. Spoon a burger-sized patty onto the hot griddle and cook until browned, about 4 minutes. Flip; add 1 slice Cheddar cheese. Cover patty with a frying pan and cook until cheese is melted, about 4 minutes more. Repeat with remaining butter, sweet potato mixture, and Cheddar cheese.
