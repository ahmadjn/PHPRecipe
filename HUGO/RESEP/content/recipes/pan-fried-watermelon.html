Easiest Way to Cook Super Quick Homemade Pan-Fried Watermelon Tasty

To get started with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have Pan-Fried Watermelon using 3 ingredients and 1 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Pan-Fried Watermelon:

  • Get 1 tablespoon butter
  • Make ready 1 small underripe watermelon, quartered and cut into 1/2-inch slices
  • Take salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps to create Pan-Fried Watermelon:

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook watermelon slices in melted butter until browned, 5 to 7 minutes per side. Season with salt and pepper to serve.
