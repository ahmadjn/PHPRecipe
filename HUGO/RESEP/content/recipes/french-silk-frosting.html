The ingredients needed to make French Silk Frosting:

  • Prepare 2 ⅔ cups confectioners' sugar
  • Make ready ⅔ cup butter, softened
  • Prepare 2 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate, melted
  • Take ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Make ready 2 tablespoons milk

Steps to make French Silk Frosting:

  1. In a medium bowl, blend confectioners sugar, butter, melted chocolate and vanilla on low speed. Increase speed to high. Gradually add milk; beat until smooth and fluffy.
