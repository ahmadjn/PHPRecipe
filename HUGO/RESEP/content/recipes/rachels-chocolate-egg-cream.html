The ingredients needed to make Rachels Chocolate Egg Cream:

  • Take ¼ cup chocolate syrup
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons milk
  • Make ready 1 cup seltzer water

Instructions to cook Rachels Chocolate Egg Cream:

  1. Mix the chocolate syrup and milk together until smooth in a large glass. Gradually pour in the seltzer water, stirring to combine (mixture will foam). Drink immediately.
