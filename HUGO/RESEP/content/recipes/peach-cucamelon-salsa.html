Steps to Make Award-winning Peach Cucamelon Salsa Delicious

To begin with this recipe, we must first prepare a few components. You can cook Peach Cucamelon Salsa using 8 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Peach Cucamelon Salsa:

  • Get ½ medium red onion, minced
  • Make ready 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • Take 2 large fresh peaches - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • Prepare ½ cup cucamelons, thinly sliced
  • Prepare 4 sprigs fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Prepare 1 green chile pepper, seeded and minced
  • Take ¼ teaspoon chili powder
  • Get 1 pinch salt

Step by Step to create Peach Cucamelon Salsa:

  1. Combine onion and lime juice in a bowl and marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Add peaches, cucamelons, cilantro, chile pepper, chili powder and salt to the bowl with the onion. Stir to combine. Chill until serving.
