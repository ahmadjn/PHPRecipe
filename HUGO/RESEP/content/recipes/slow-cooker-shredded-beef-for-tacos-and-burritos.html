To get started with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few ingredients. You can have Slow Cooker Shredded Beef for Tacos and Burritos using 4 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Shredded Beef for Tacos and Burritos:

  • Prepare 1 (4 pound) boneless beef chuck roast
  • Make ready 2 (1 ounce) packages taco seasoning
  • Take 1 onion, halved and sliced
  • Make ready 1 teaspoon seasoned salt, or more to taste

Steps to cook Slow Cooker Shredded Beef for Tacos and Burritos:

  1. Place beef roast in crock of a slow cooker. Sprinkle taco seasoning over the beef; top with onion slices.
  2. Cook on Low 8 to 10 hours.
  3. Remove beef to a cutting board and shred with a pair of forks. Return beef to slow cooker and season with seasoned salt.
