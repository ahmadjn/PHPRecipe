The ingredients needed to make Savory Oregano Salami:

  • Take 1 pound salami, cut into bite-size pieces
  • Prepare 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Make ready 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • Prepare 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Prepare ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese, or more to taste
  • Make ready 1 ½ teaspoons chopped oregano
  • Prepare 4 sprigs parsley

Step by Step to make Savory Oregano Salami:

  1. Place salami in a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Drizzle with olive oil; season with salt and pepper. Cook until grill marks appear, 5 to 7 minutes. Flip; cook until second side is browned, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Place salami in a small bowl; mix in Parmesan cheese and oregano. Garnish with parsley.
