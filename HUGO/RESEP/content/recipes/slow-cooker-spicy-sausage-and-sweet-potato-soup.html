Recipe of Speedy Slow Cooker Spicy Sausage and Sweet Potato Soup Tasty

To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can have Slow Cooker Spicy Sausage and Sweet Potato Soup using 9 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Spicy Sausage and Sweet Potato Soup:

  • Prepare 3 mild Italian sausage links, casings removed
  • Take 2 hot Italian sausage links, casings removed
  • Make ready 3 (14 ounce) cans low-sodium chicken broth
  • Make ready 4 small sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • Get 3 tablespoons Carribbean jerk marinade, or to taste
  • Get 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Get ½ teaspoon ground thyme
  • Make ready 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • Prepare 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained

Steps to cook Slow Cooker Spicy Sausage and Sweet Potato Soup:

  1. Slice sausage links into 1/2-inch chunks. Cook sausages in a skillet over high heat until browned but not cooked through, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove and pat dry with paper towels to remove excess fat. Transfer to a slow cooker and add chicken broth, sweet potatoes, jerk marinade, garlic, thyme, and cinnamon.
  2. Cook on High for 3 hours. Add black beans, reduce heat to Low, and cook for an additional 2 hours.
