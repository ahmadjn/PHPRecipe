Recipe of Quick Easy Chicken Enchilada Soup Delicious

To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook Easy Chicken Enchilada Soup using 11 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Easy Chicken Enchilada Soup:

  • Prepare 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • Take 2 (14 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Take 2 cups chicken stock
  • Get 1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • Prepare 1 (14 ounce) can fire-roasted diced tomatoes, with juice
  • Get 1 (10 ounce) can enchilada sauce
  • Make ready 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
  • Take 1 white onion, diced
  • Take 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Take 1 teaspoon salt
  • Make ready 1 teaspoon ground cumin

Step by Step to create Easy Chicken Enchilada Soup:

  1. Combine chicken breasts, black beans, chicken stock, corn, tomatoes, enchilada sauce, green chiles, onion, garlic, salt, and cumin in a multi-functional pressure cooker. Close and lock the lid. Select Stew mode and cook on high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 30 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  2. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, 10 to 40 minutes. Remove chicken, shred it with 2 forks, and return it to the soup.
