Recipe of Perfect Smoker Sauce Tasty

To begin with this recipe, we must first prepare a few components. You can have Smoker Sauce using 8 ingredients and 1 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Smoker Sauce:

  • Prepare 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce
  • Prepare ½ (8 ounce) jar salsa
  • Take ½ cup soy sauce
  • Prepare ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • Make ready 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Prepare 1 tablespoon crushed garlic
  • Get 1 tablespoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • Make ready salt to taste

Step by Step to create Smoker Sauce:

  1. In a large bowl, stir together the barbeque sauce, salsa, soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, garlic, ground black pepper, and salt. Store in a jar in the refrigerator until ready to use. Use as a marinade or a basting sauce when smoking meats or fish.
