Step-by-Step Guide to Make Any-night-of-the-week Chinese Dan-Bin Delicious

To begin with this recipe, we must first prepare a few components. You can cook Chinese Dan-Bin using 5 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Chinese Dan-Bin:

  • Get 1 slice bacon
  • Take 1 egg, beaten
  • Take 1 (6 inch) corn tortilla
  • Take ¼ cup sweet corn kernels
  • Make ready 1 tablespoon smooth salsa

Steps to make Chinese Dan-Bin:

  1. Fry the slice of bacon in a skillet over medium-high heat. Set aside. Reduce the heat to medium. Pour the egg into the skillet, and sprinkle with corn. Cook for less than 10 seconds.
  2. Lay the tortilla over the whole egg. After the egg is firm and attached to the tortilla, flip over, and lay the bacon on top. Put salsa over the bacon, and wrap. Remove from the skillet, cut into small pieces and serve hot.
