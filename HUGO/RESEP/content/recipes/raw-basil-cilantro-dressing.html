The ingredients needed to make Raw Basil Cilantro Dressing:

  • Prepare ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Get ⅓ cup apple cider vinegar
  • Make ready ⅓ cup chopped fresh basil, or more to taste
  • Take ⅓ cup chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste
  • Prepare 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • Get 2 teaspoons liquid aminos
  • Prepare Himalayan pink salt to taste
  • Make ready cracked black pepper to taste

Step by Step to cook Raw Basil Cilantro Dressing:

  1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, basil, cilantro, nutritional yeast, liquid aminos, salt, and pepper together in a bowl until smooth.
