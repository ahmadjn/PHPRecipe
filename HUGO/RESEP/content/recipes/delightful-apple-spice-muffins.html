To begin with this particular recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can cook Delightful Apple Spice Muffins using 9 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Delightful Apple Spice Muffins:

  • Get ½ cup margarine
  • Get ⅔ cup white sugar
  • Take 2 eggs
  • Prepare 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • Make ready 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Get 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • Make ready 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Make ready ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • Prepare 1 cup applesauce

Instructions to create Delightful Apple Spice Muffins:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F (175 degrees C). Line a mini muffin pan with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter or margarine and sugar. Add eggs, and beat until smooth. Blend in cinnamon, allspice, baking powder, and baking soda. Alternately add in a half cup of applesauce, one cup of flour, remaining applesauce, and remaining flour. Stir until just blended together. Pour batter into muffin cups 2/3's of the way full.
  3. Bake in preheated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) oven for 17 minutes.
