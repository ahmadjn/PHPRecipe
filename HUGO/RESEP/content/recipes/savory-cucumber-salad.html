The ingredients needed to make Savory Cucumber Salad:

  • Prepare 3 large cucumbers, sliced
  • Take ⅛ teaspoon salt, or as needed
  • Make ready 1 ¼ cups sour cream
  • Make ready ¾ bunch green onions (white and pale green parts only), chopped
  • Prepare ¼ cup white sugar
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • Make ready 2 cloves garlic, or more to taste, minced
  • Take ¼ teaspoon dried dill weed
  • Take ⅛ teaspoon paprika
  • Prepare ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • Take ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

Step by Step to create Savory Cucumber Salad:

  1. Spread a paper towel over a large platter. Arrange cucumber slices in a single layer atop the paper towel. Sprinkle 1/8 teaspoon salt over the cucumber slices; refrigerate at least 2 hours and up to 6 hours.
  2. Mix sour cream, green onions, white sugar, apple cider vinegar, garlic, dill, paprika, 1/8 teaspoon salt, and black pepper together in a bowl; refrigerate at least 1 hour and up to 4 hours.
  3. Pour sour cream sauce over the cucumbers and stir gently to coat.
