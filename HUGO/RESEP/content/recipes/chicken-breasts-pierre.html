Recipe of Speedy Chicken Breasts Pierre Delicious

To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few components. You can cook Chicken Breasts Pierre using 16 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Chicken Breasts Pierre:

  • Make ready 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • Prepare ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • Prepare ½ teaspoon salt
  • Get 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • Get 3 tablespoons butter
  • Make ready 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, with liquid
  • Prepare ½ cup water
  • Take 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
  • Take 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • Get 1 teaspoon salt
  • Get 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • Get 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • Get ½ teaspoon celery seed
  • Prepare 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Get ⅛ teaspoon hot pepper sauce

Steps to make Chicken Breasts Pierre:

  1. In a shallow dish or bowl, combine flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt and ground black pepper. Coat chicken breasts with flour mixture. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat, and brown chicken on all sides. Remove from skillet, and drain on paper towels.
  2. In the same skillet, combine the tomatoes, water, brown sugar, vinegar and Worcestershire sauce. Season with salt, chili powder, mustard, celery seed, garlic and hot pepper sauce. Bring to a boil; reduce heat, and return chicken to skillet. Cover, and simmer for 35 to 40 minutes, or until chicken is tender, no longer pink and juices run clear.
