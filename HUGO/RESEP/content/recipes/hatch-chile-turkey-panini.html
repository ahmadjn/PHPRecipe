Recipe of Ultimate Hatch Chile Turkey Panini Tasty

To get started with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can have Hatch Chile Turkey Panini using 6 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Hatch Chile Turkey Panini:

  • Prepare 8 ounces Dietz & Watson Hatch Chile Turkey
  • Take 3 ounces Dietz & Watson Hatch Chile Cheese
  • Make ready 2 slices white bread
  • Take ½ cup wilted spinach
  • Make ready ¼ cup julienned red onion
  • Get 2 shishito peppers

Step by Step to make Hatch Chile Turkey Panini:

  1. Layer your ingredients as follows: bread, spinach, Dietz &amp; Watson Hatch Chile Cheese, Dietz &amp; Watson Hatch Turkey, red onion, and bread.
  2. Place the panini on a panini press for 5 minutes.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Grill the shishito peppers until blistered, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  4. Plate the panini with the peppers.
