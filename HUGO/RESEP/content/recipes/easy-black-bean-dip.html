The ingredients needed to make Easy Black Bean Dip:

  • Get 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Get 1 ½ tablespoons water, or more if needed
  • Get 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Prepare 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Get 1 teaspoon ground cumin, or more to taste
  • Take 1 teaspoon chili powder, or more to taste
  • Make ready 1 teaspoon onion powder, or more to taste
  • Prepare ½ teaspoon salt

Instructions to make Easy Black Bean Dip:

  1. Combine beans, water, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, chili powder, onion powder, and salt in a food processor; process until thick and smooth, adding more water if needed to reach desired thickness.
  2. Chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
