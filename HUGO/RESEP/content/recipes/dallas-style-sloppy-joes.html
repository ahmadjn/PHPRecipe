Steps to Cook Quick Dallas-Style Sloppy Joes Tasty

To get started with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook Dallas-Style Sloppy Joes using 12 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Dallas-Style Sloppy Joes:

  • Make ready 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • Take 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • Make ready 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • Get sea salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Make ready 1 ½ cups ketchup
  • Take 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • Make ready 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • Make ready 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • Prepare 3 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • Prepare 3 tablespoons hickory flavored barbecue sauce
  • Prepare 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • Prepare 5 large hamburger buns, toasted

Steps to create Dallas-Style Sloppy Joes:

  1. Cook the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until completely browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Add the onion and bell pepper, season with sea salt and black pepper, and cook until vegetables soften, about 7 minutes.
  2. Stir in the ketchup, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, mustard, and barbeque sauce. Reduce heat to low and simmer the mixture until thickened, about 10 minutes. Add Parmesan cheese and serve on toasted hamburger buns.
