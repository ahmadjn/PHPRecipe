Step-by-Step Guide to Prepare Ultimate Jens Greek Couscous Salad Tasty

To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can have Jens Greek Couscous Salad using 10 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Jens Greek Couscous Salad:

  • Prepare 2 ½ cups water
  • Get 2 cups Israeli couscous
  • Make ready 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1/4-by-2-inch strips
  • Get 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1/4-by-2-inch strips
  • Prepare ½ cup olive oil
  • Get lemon, juiced
  • Make ready salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Get 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Take 1 cup kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • Make ready ½ cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps to create Jens Greek Couscous Salad:

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan; add couscous and return mixture to a boil. Place a lid on the saucepan, reduce heat to low, and cook at a simmer until couscous is tender and the water is absorbed, about 10 minutes. Transfer couscous to a bowl.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Cook red bell pepper and yellow bell pepper strips in the hot skillet until soft and slightly charred, about 5 minutes; add to couscous and stir.
  3. Whisk olive oil and lemon juice together in a bowl; season with salt and pepper. Drizzle oil mixture over the couscous mixture and stir to coat. Gently stir tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese through the couscous.
